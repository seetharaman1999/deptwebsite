Pre-placement training is given to students to make them ‘Plug & Play’ in industry in the areas of Technical knowledge. We also have the potential to place 100% of the eligible students

Placement training

It nurtures Industry-Institute interaction and moulds and mentors the students to be fit for placement. It also provides job opportunities by bringing companies for campus recruitment to all eligible students of the final year. It enhances and employs current practical and technical knowledge of the students. Added to that, it makes the students plug and play by imparting soft skills and employability skills. It networks with Industries and other Professional Societies to enter into MOUs and also maintains Overseas Relations of the Institution handling issues like Student Recruitment etc.

Buisness English Certificate

Training Programmes are conducted once every year for the Buisness English Certificte(BEC) which is provided by the University of Cambridge. The programme innovates and inculcates the language with more of buisness related terms. Undertaking this course helps students in improving their language related with buisness.

Aptitude Training

Aptitude Training Programme is conducted every semester in order to enhance the aptitude and problem solving skills of the students which aid them in placement. The programme also aims in developing the english knowledge of the students. The training is aimed at increasing the problem solving ability of students and to help them minimize the time consumption while doing so and also in developing thier english. This helps the students in the aptitude round of placements.

Value Added Course

Value added courses are proivded to the students at regular periods to help studens enhance various abilities which they will have to develop during the time of their interviews. The value added courses aim at teaching students content which are out of the syllabus and are in current trends. The course aims at providing the students with knowledge they must have at that insant of time.

Mock Interview

This is a training programme which is conducted in order to give the students a experience of how an interview would be. The student gets real time experience of how interviews are conducted and thus learns how to respond to various situations during an interview.

eBOX

The eBOX training is conducted once every year to enhave the students' skills in the C programming language. Every year trainers are invited to interact with the studets and improv their knowladge in C programming
