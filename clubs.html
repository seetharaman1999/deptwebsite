Cognizant Student CLub

The Cognizant Student Club is launched to bridge the gap between the college students and the corporate. Pre-final year is the right time for enhancing skills and preparing for placement. In this regard, Cognizant student club will facilitates development of skills and competency.

Objectives of the club

  • To develop the technical knowledge and skills of the students.
  • To enhance the communication and interpersonal skills of the students.
  • To provide a holistic personality development.
  • To provide corporate and Industry Exposure.

For more details visit the club blog.

Gnu Linux Users Club (GLUG)



The GNU Linux Users Group is a student hosted group (GLUG) which was first formed by batch of 2014 with the guidance of Mr.Prakash, the Vice President of Free Software Foundation of Tamil Nadu (FSFTN). It is a club which encourages the usage of free and open source software and the open source operating system LINUX among the students. The club meets up once every week and in every session a concept which is beyond the syllabus and which is less preaveled among student is thought to student. The club is inaugurated every year for the the second year and anyone can become the member of this club.For, more information visit the club's blog.
