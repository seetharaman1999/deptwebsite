Saturday Activities

The college encourages the students to conduct various technical & non-technical activities at the classroom level, to inspire them to organise the activities and participate in them. This creates a friendly atmosphere where everyone participates and performs better, thereby adding to their progress. It motivates the students to improve their leadership quality. Apart from this, the students are also taken to various NGO's, thus inculcating a serving nature in the students. Saturday activities include visiting Non Governmental Organization(NGO).
