Activities

Learning is made a lot easier and interesting through various activities, like industrial visits, guest lectures and various technical activities. This inculcates the urge to gain more knowledge in students thereby making them better professionals to face the modern day challenges. The students are exposed to the current industrial atmosphere which enables them to grasp more than their academic syllabus. Our college focuses on fulfilling the dream of every student by providing all the necessary state-of-the-art facilities to achieve in their areas of interest. St. Joseph’s offers the best opportunities to the students to achieve in curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

InnovITy

"We Customize The Future" The confluence of innovation and technology gives out InnovitZ. Yes,we Innovate in the field of Information technology. Our IT club has been innovating continuously for the past couple of years. The student and staff members of the IT club create events in regard to improving the standard of technology information that is given out to the students. Students of the IT club pursue their thirst for technology by quizzing, coding, debugging, googly... etc for which they are rewarded handsomely as well. The students are free to use the lab and the guidance of the expert faculty members to create or customize a software for the present industrial problems. Join our club to experience it.
